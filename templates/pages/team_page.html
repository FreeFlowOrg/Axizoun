Job Vacancies

Axizoun is an online recruiting platform that offers an innovative approach to recruiting. We minimize the traditional recruiting practices by using innovative testing approaches. Applicants are required to build their profile using a list of skill sets which are then matched with potential jobs that require these skillsets. This helps recruiters get a finer search result for whom they need to interview. The trump card is what follows next. Applicants can only apply for a position after they solve a business question that tests their skillsets that they need for the job. This has a two-fold effect. 1 Recruiters are able to have a pre-screening process and thereby only invite a few skillful candidates for further screening. 2. There are no prejudices or biases from this initial screening process. This is because everyone across the board is compared based on the solution they offered and not based on what university they come from or who they know in that company. Employer: we provide a continuous stream of talent to employers in a completely unbiased and effective manner. We will ensure that the by reversing the funnel you will only interview those candidates that perfectly fit the job requirements and will succeed in that job position. Applicants: We at Axizoun offer you a platform to give you a chance to work for any company by competing with others on the same problem. While on the other hand the employee looking to hire you would completely base their judgment on your working abilities based on your solutions to the problem. We at Axizoun believe that it’s high time we stop listening to people saying, “it’s not about what you know, it’s about who you know”. Because at Axizoun we will provide you the opportunity to achieve your goals because you deserve it and not because someone is handing it to you. Join us to be a part of a change. Join us to be a part of the future.
